The Finances of California
and other useful data.

  • Background Information

    • There has been a recent financial dilemma within the United States wherein financial growth has occurred at a noticeable rate yet has been disproportionally distributed within the country. This distribution is said to be uneven in terms of economic class and gender and there exists countless articles claiming various things are to blame for this dilemma, but most seem to provide little data to support these claims. Even when data is provided, it is often in too large or too small of a scope to be used in a meaningful way; the context is purely in the perspective of the country or very specific areas, which often leaves misunderstanding. This websites's purpose is to allow easier access to California's financial data and allow a wider audience an oppertunity to find their own causes of this problem. This issue is complex and not one answer will ever be the right one, but hopefully through easily accessible data this puzzle can be pieced together.
