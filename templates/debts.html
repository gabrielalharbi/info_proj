    • Debt Distribution: The debt distribution tab contains a pie chart of the 4 major debt types in California, as well as the average amount for each debt. This chart can be useful in understanding the financial well-being of the state's citizens. If they have huge median wages, but even higher debts then there is obviously a problem. It is important to note that while the mortgage is high, the average Californian might not have a mortgage (though the average mortgage is higher than the average at a country wide level).


    Data: The data table contains the data for the debt distribution chart as well as the amount of bankruptcies filed in the last year. This data can easily be compared to the data found on other tables.
