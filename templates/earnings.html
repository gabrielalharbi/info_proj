    • Male & Female Median Wage: This chart graphs the median wage of the average man and woman on a by county basis. What is interesting to note is that even though--not taking into consideration jobs--women on average make 90% of what men make, men only have a higher median wage in 16 out of 58 counties. This is known as Simpson's paradox and is most likely a result of men having higher median wages in larger counties.
